About

Education

I completed my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering from Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Vellore, India. After that, I graduated from Purdue University, West Lafayette, USA, with a Master of Science (MS) degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering with specialization in VLSI and Circuit Design. At Purdue, I was in the thesis track.

Research Work

At Purdue, my research work focussed on compensating errors due to systematic hardware non-idealities present in the crossbar arrays of In-Memory Computing (IMC) based DNN accelerators and recouping inference accuracy. I worked on error compensation both at the crossbar level as well as layer level for both high precision DNNs as well as Binary Neural Networks.

Industry Experience

After completing my undergrad from VIT, I worked as a Software Developer in SAP Labs, Bangalore, India for two years which was my first exposure to the corporate world. Over the course of these two years, I honed my coding skills in various programing languages like Java and ABAP. After graduating from Purdue, I am currently working in Samsung SARC/ACL as a Senior Engineer.

Interests

In my free time, I like to learn about topics that are outside my knowledge circle like health and wellness, music, literature, finance etc. I like travelling and have spent a good amount of time visiting many tourist attractions in India.
